Bedrock Quota Dashboard

Why this dashboard?

This dashboard closes the six gaps that make Bedrock quota consumption monitoring hard.

1🔀

Quotas & metrics live apart

Limits sit in Service Quotas, consumption in CloudWatch — cross-referenced by hand, one model at a time.

2🗺️

The mapping is undocumented

No canonical map ties a CloudWatch ModelId to its Service Quotas QuotaCode.

3📊

No unified multi-model view

Nothing shows every model’s TPM usage side-by-side with its limit. You get metrics or quotas — not both.

4🧩

Three quota types, three limits

On-Demand, Cross-Region and Global each draw down separate quotas — and it’s non-obvious which is which.

5🌎

Multi-region monitoring complexity

Cross-region & global profiles span multiple regions at once. CloudWatch can show them together, but only by wiring up a cross-region dashboard manually.

6🚨

Found out at throttle time

Without an easy way to watch headroom, exhaustion is usually discovered in production when requests start failing.

➜
One static page that solves all six. Quotas + live usage, every model × region, in one view.
